Flash Channels Integration

An IOTA Flash Channel is established between two WiFiota users, after the WiFi connection between the devices has been established. Every minute of client data usage is associated with a token transfer in the channel.


Signing a transfer signifies agreement to pay the billed amount. Limits are enforced via specified buy/sell min/max values set by each user. The equal funding of the channel ensures shared risk between both parties, insuring against a user leaving the channel early.


Checks are implemented to monitor if time sensitive constraints were not fulfilled, such as the channel not being funded, no peers, etc.
